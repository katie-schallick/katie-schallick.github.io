neuropulse

  •  Brand Creation
  •  Print Design
  •  Brand Management

Through biometric data, Neuropulse is a program that seamlessly uncovers truth. As a new strategic offering from Hyperquake, the Neuropulse brand had to feel unique while related to the Hyperquake brand. Precise lines of data interlace over organic shapes of human engagement to create the Neuropulse visual language illustrating the transition of observations into insights.
